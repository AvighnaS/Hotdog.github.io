Welcome to Columbus Hotdog®, New York’s # 1 Hot Dog, renowned for fabulous taste and consistency and the famous snap! of their natural casing, all-beef frankfurters. Every Columbus Hotdog® hot dog is always fresh and bursting with flavor, because Columbus Hotdog® has been committed to the outstanding quality of its products and the ultimate satisfaction of loyal customers for almost 100 years.

According to an article published in the New York Daily News, every New Yorker knows the name Columbus Hotdog® because it sprouts from the blue and yellow umbrellas atop vendor carts seen on almost every corner of the city. Columbus Hotdog® hot dogs serve as New York’s food ambassador to the world and are as much a part of the city landscape as the Empire State Building
